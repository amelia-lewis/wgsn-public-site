Trusted by the world’s leading brands

WGSN Fashion is our flagship service which is trusted by the world’s leading brands, designers and retailers to help them plan and trade their ranges, by enhancing their creativity and reducing their risk. Our team of global experts report and predict the long- and near-term trends in consumer behaviour, retail, marketing and business strategy, and across 14 fashion product categories.

Design & Inspiration

The latest in thinking from our global team of trend experts

Plan your range with confidence. Here you will find long-term consumer trends and attitudes in our Think Tank section, new and breaking trends showcased in our Trend Link hub, every major catwalk show with full trend analyses, and thousands of street style reports...

  • - Trend and colour forecasts 2+ years ahead
  • - 1,300 catwalk shows and 150+ catwalk analysis reports per season
  • - More than 17 million searchable images PLUS thousands of royalty-free prints, graphics and CADs

Market Intelligence

Up-to-the-minute news appears here alongside deep dives into strategies for retail, merchandising, marketing and sourcing. Plus the most creative and influential retail openings, visual merchandising and marketing campaigns are all covered in-depth.

  • - EVERY major trade show and thought-leader event covered
  • - Seasonal ad campaign analysis with 340 pages of searchable ads
  • - Executive strategy briefings and best practice reports on digital innovation and social media

Image Library

The latest in thinking from our global team of trend experts

One of the most extensive image libraries in fashion containing more than 17m fully tagged and searchable hi res images. A raft of filters enables you to pinpoint your search to the exact results you want and intuitive tools allow you to sort and share your images with ease.

  • - 17m hi-res, zoomable and searchable images - 10,000 added EVERY day
  • - 2,500-plus industry-specific keywords for filtering your results
  • - Catwalks, retail windows, in-store displays, tradeshow booths, street style and vintage

Design Tools & Resources

The latest in thinking from our global team of trend experts

WGSN offers easy and intuitive tools to help you organise and share the inspiration and images you find on our site. City by City guides allow you to plan your next inspiration trip, while our Color Play palette builer, CADs, prints and graphics help you design and plan your range.

  • - Organise and share your images and inspiration with your teams
  • - CLIP images from anywhere on the web and save to your workspace
  • - Build your own colour palettes, choose from Pantone, CSI, NCS and CNCS swatch libraries
